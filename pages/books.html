Book News

Artemis the new book from the author who previously penned the Martian is unfortunately a bit of a miss on the hit and miss side of things. We've picked a review from the avclub which adequately describes the faults of the book, honestly thought you'd be better off to skip this one. On a brighter note the Brandan Sanderson novel Oathbringer the third book in the epic The Stormlight Archive series has finally been released and well its classic Sanderson good fantasy worldbuilding and plot plus cheesy dialogue, if you like how he writes, well then this is for you and if you don't like his writing this book will not do anything to change your mind on him.

Avclub is killing it recently with some fantastic article worth pointing out such as their top comics of 2017, a comprehensive guide to the best comics of the year. Although we personally think that David Rubins Beowolf is the comic you should focus all your attention on. Finally its also worth having a read of the avclub article on the differences between the film Die Hard and the book it was based on, definitely an interesting read all round.
