Tv News

Tv continues on an upward trajectory of ever improving quality, and leading the pack is of course our pick of the season Mr. Robot. Its hard to describe the value of this show to the average viewer however if you are interested in tv that consistently tries to push the boundaries and plays with expectations then we are certain that this is the show for you. Following the main lead down the rabbit hole of conspiracy, paranoia and competing corporate interests makes this show a nail biter combined with a stark style that reels you in to the atmosphere of the show, which keeps us coming back for more, Mr. Robot is in with an easy shot of winning show of the year.

As winter approaches we see the currently airing wrapping up either to end their seasons or simply take a mid-season break. There are quite a few that have caught our attention this year such as the South Park finale, a show that after twenty or so seasons remains on top of its game with biting satirical humour on a whole range of political topics. Lucifer also takes a mid-season break after resolving its latest arc on the who the mysterious criminal known as the sinner man is?

Finally, another show of interest, the Simpsons has its Christmas episode that is surprisingly better than modern Simpsons has offered in quite some time, it features the return of psychotic killer sideshow Bob, a fan favourite character so at any rate it's worth a look.
